✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth awakes,

As Biratnagar basks in sun's fiery makes.

Aalborg's chill whispers low, with grey skies wept,

A world of difference, in each city's step.

The scent of wet earth rises in Kathmandu's air,

While Biratnagar's heat brings forth the scent of blooming flair.

Aalborg's misty veil, a somber mood does bring,

But Kathmandu's mild light, my heart does sing.
