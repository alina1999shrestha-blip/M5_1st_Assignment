✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth whispers low,

A soothing calm that beckons, as the sun's soft glow

Dances upon the rooftops, with a gentle, loving touch.

In Biratnagar, the air is thick with heat's fierce clutch,

The wind a gentle lover's caress, that brings a scent of bloom.

Aalborg, shrouded in grey, its chill begins to seep,

The relentless wind a mournful sigh, that whispers through the trees.

Kathmandu's mild embrace, a haven from the extremes, it seems,

A place to bask in gentle warmth, and let the spirit breathe.

The scent of blooming flowers, and the soft rustle of leaves,

Invite you to linger, in this serene and peaceful haven.
