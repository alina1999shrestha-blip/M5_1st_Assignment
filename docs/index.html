✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As morning dawns, Kathmandu's chill begins to fade,

A gentle breeze stirs, whispers through the shade.

Biratnagar swelters, warm and dry, with winds that roam,

While Aalborg's crisp air carries the scent of a distant home.

The Danish city's sky, a pale and serene sight,

Invites the sun to dance, with clouds that drift in light.

In contrast, Kathmandu's clouds congregate and grey,

Bringing life-giving rain, to soothe the city's dusty way.

Biratnagar's heat, though intense, may leave one feeling worn,

But Aalborg's mild warmth, and gentle breeze, make it the place to be born.
