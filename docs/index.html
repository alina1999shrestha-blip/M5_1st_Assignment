✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu, the sky is veiled in grey,

A soothing breeze whispers through the day,

The scent of wet earth rises high,

As petals lift to meet the gentle sky.

In Biratnagar, the sun beats down with might,

A warmer breeze stirs, carrying scents of night,

While Aalborg's horizon stretches wide and bright,

With a crisp chill in the air, a refreshing sight.

The warmth of Biratnagar may appeal to some,

But Aalborg's gentle chill is where I'd prefer to roam,

For in its airy light, my spirit would revive,

And the world would feel alive.
