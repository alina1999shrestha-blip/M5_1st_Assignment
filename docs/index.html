✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the breeze whispers low,

A soothing calm, as clouds envelop the city's glow.

Biratnagar basks in sweltering heat, with skies so grey,

Aalborg's openness beckons, with sunshine's warm ray.

The chill of night in Aalborg's air, a refreshing treat,

Contrasts with Biratnagar's warmth, where heat can't be beat.

Kathmandu's scent of wet earth rises, as rain falls deep,

Aalborg's gentle warmth and light, my heart doth keep.
