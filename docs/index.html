✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth awakes,

As Biratnagar's skies, with clouds, heavy rains make.

Aalborg's chill, a striking contrast bears,

With winds that whisper, through its airy squares.

The scent of wet earth rises, in Biratnagar's air,

While Kathmandu's light, with gentle warmth, is fair.

Aalborg's dry sky, a serene sight to see,

Makes Kathmandu, the nicest, for you and me.
