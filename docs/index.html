✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As morning breaks, Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

with clouds that veil the sun, and petals of rain that flow.

In Biratnagar, warmth ascends, a fiery spirit that gleams,

under skies of scattered clouds, where tropical scents beam.

Aalborg's chill awakens, with winds that cut like a knife,

and grey skies weeping softly, with a melancholy life.

The air is crisp and cool, with a scent of dampened earth,

in Aalborg's autumnal grasp, where nature's palette gives birth.

But if I were to choose, I'd bask in Kathmandu's mild light,

where the warmth and gentle showers dance, on this serene night.
