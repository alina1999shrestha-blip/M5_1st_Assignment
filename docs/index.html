✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, a whisper of rain falls deep,

With warmth and chill in balance, the day's sweet scent does creep.

Biratnagar's sweltering heat, a fiery blaze that never fades,

Contrasts with Aalborg's grey skies, where chill winds whisper shades.

The Danish city's air is crisp, with clouds that veil the light,

While Biratnagar's open sky shines bright, with a burning, fierce delight.

Kathmandu's mild and tranquil, a haven from the heat and grey,

A place to breathe, to live, to be, on this gentle, summer's day.
