✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's shadowy veil, the breeze whispers low,

A soothing melody, as clouds bestow

A gentle chill, a scent of wet earth rise,

A somber sky, with precipitation's sighs.

In Biratnagar, warmth awakens, wild and free,

A lighter sky, with clouds that drift carelessly,

The wind stirs strong, with a gentle, rustling sound.

Aalborg's sky, a canvas, painted with a gentle hand,

With warmth and light, that dances, unbound.

The city's gentle warmth, and cloudless sky, make it a haven, tomorrow, to lie.
