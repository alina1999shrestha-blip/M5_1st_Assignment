In Kathmandu's misty veil, the air is warm and light,

A gentle breeze stirs, with scents of blooming night.

In Biratnagar, the sun beats down with fervent might,

Warming the skin, beneath a sky of open sight.

In Aalborg, chill winds whisper through the grey,

As clouds congregate, and rain falls, day by day.

The warmth of Biratnagar beckons, a haven from the chill,

A place to bask in sunlight, and let the spirit fill.

The gentle warmth and open sky make Biratnagar the place,

To soak in the sun's radiance, and let the heart find its space.

The city's warmth and light, a haven from the cloudy grey,

Invite the soul to linger, on this sunny day.
