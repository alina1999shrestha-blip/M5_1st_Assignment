✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As morning breaks, Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

with clouds that veil the sun, and a soft, serene glow.

In Biratnagar, warmth awakens, a vibrant, lively beat,

where sunbeams dance upon the earth, and heat rises to greet.

Aalborg's chill, crisp air carries the scent of dampened earth,

a somber sky, with clouds that weep, yet no precipitation's birth.

The warmth of Biratnagar's breeze, or Kathmandu's mild, calm light,

makes either city a haven, but Biratnagar's warmth feels just right.
