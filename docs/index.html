✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu, a gentle breeze whispers low,

as sunlight filters through a partly cloudy glow.

In Biratnagar, the air is thick with heat and light,

a scorching warmth that beckons through the day and night.

Aalborg, in contrast, is shrouded in a chill grey haze,

with winds that howl and whip the face in disarray.

The scent of blooms in Kathmandu's mild air is sweet,

making it the city where I'd love to take a seat.

The warmth of Biratnagar may appeal to some, but I

prefer the gentle balance that Kathmandu's skies imply.
