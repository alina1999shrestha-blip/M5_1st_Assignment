✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth awakes,

As Biratnagar's skies burn with a fiercer heat that makes,

The air thick with an impending storm's dark shade,

Contrasting Aalborg's chill, where winds whisper through the glade.

The scent of wet earth rises in Kathmandu's mild air,

While Biratnagar's dry heat beckons, beyond the clouds' thin lair,

Aalborg's grey skies weep with a soothing, gentle hush,

In Kathmandu, the balance of warmth and chill, I'd love to rush.
