✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the scent of rain unfolds,

As petals lift to greet the misty morning's hold.

Biratnagar's warmer skies, a vibrant tapestry display,

With sunbeams dancing, as the wind whispers through the day.

Aalborg's chill awakens, with a softer, greyer light,

A contrast to the warmth that Biratnagar's skies ignite.

The warmth of Biratnagar's sun-kissed streets, I'd love to roam,

And bask in its radiant glow, where warmth and light call home.
