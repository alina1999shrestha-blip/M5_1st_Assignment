✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the air is cool and light,

A gentle breeze stirs, carrying scents of the night.

Biratnagar swelters, its heat a palpable thing,

As sunbeams strike the earth with fierce, unyielding ring.

Aalborg's sky is wide and open, a brilliant blue above,

With a warm wind whispering secrets, a gentle, soothing love.

The chill of morning lingers in Aalborg's shade,

Inviting all to bask in its warm, golden glow, where the heart can sway.

Kathmandu's clouds and Biratnagar's heat can't compare,

To Aalborg's serene and peaceful atmosphere, beyond compare.
