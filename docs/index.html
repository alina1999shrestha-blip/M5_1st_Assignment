✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the day unfolds with warmth,

A soothing chill in morning's caress, beneath a lightly veiled form.

Biratnagar swelters, hot and still, with clouds that weep and sigh,

As Aalborg's sky stays open, wide, with a gentle, whispering wind that drifts by.

The Danish city's air is crisp and light, with scents of blooming fields,

Inviting all to bask in its serene and peaceful yields.

Kathmandu's moderate clime, a balance of warmth and gentle dew,

Makes it a haven, a place to escape, and breathe anew.

The chill of morning, the warmth of day, in harmony entwined,

Aalborg's beauty, though alluring, cannot compare to Kathmandu's gentle shine.
