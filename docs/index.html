✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's hills, a gentle breeze whispers low,

with clouds that veil the sky, and precipitation's soft flow.

Biratnagar's plains, a warmer clime does hold,

where sunshine pours, and heat does unfold.

Aalborg's chill, a crisp air does impart,

with winds that sweep, and a clouded heart.

The warmth of Biratnagar's sun-kissed ground,

invites me to bask, without a sound.

The gentle chill of Kathmandu's morning dew,

is a close second, but Biratnagar's warmth shines through.

The city's vibrant pulse, a sensory delight,

makes it the nicest to be in, on this coming night.
