In Kathmandu, a gentle breeze whispers low,

as warmth and chill in harmony bestow,

a soothing balance that the senses greet,

under skies with clouds that softly meet.

In Biratnagar, the air is thick with heat,

and scents of wet earth rise to greet the street,

the wind stirs strong, with precipitation's beat.

In Aalborg, a chill begins to seep,

with cloudy skies that darkness softly keep.

The warmth of Kathmandu's gentle clime,

seems the most inviting, a peaceful time.
