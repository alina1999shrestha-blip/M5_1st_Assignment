✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the morning sun will rise,

With whispers of a chill, and a sky of cloudy surprise.

Biratnagar's warmth will beckon, with a scent of wet earth sweet,

As rain drums on the rooftops, in a rhythmic, relentless beat.

Aalborg's grey skies will weep, with a wind that cuts like a knife,

And a chill that seeps into the bones, with a harsh, biting life.

But if I had to choose, I'd bask in Biratnagar's warm, rainy night,

For in its sensual, scented air, I'd find a peaceful, sweet delight.
