✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

Aalborg's chill begins to show,

Biratnagar's warmth shines bright and bold,

A tale of three cities, young and old.

The Himalayan town's mild temperature sway,

Invites the heart to come and stay,

While Aalborg's wind and cloudless grey,

Make Biratnagar's sunshine the best way.

The choice is clear, the heat to abide,

In Biratnagar's warm and sunny side,

Where clouds do part and skies are wide.
