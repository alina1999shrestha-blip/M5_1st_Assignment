In Kathmandu's misty veil, the air is warm and still,

A soothing calm that whispers through the hill.

As clouds converge, and rain drums out a soothing ease.

Aalborg, in contrast, stands beneath a brilliant sky,

While Aalborg's breeze carries the hint of a distant sea's flair.

The warmth of Biratnagar's night may be alluring to some,

But Aalborg's crystal light and crispness make it a haven to roam.
