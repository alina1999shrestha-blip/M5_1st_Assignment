✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the sun's warm touch is faint,

A gentle breeze stirs, with scents of wet earth and rain.

Biratnagar's skies are darker, with winds that whisper low,

A hotter, more turbulent air, with clouds that heavily flow.

Aalborg's chill is crisp and clean, with winds that sweep the shore,

A sunny sky with clouds that drift, and a gentle breeze once more.

The warmth of Kathmandu's evening, with a softer, quieter night,

Might be the gentlest haven, a peaceful, soothing sight.
