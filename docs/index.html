✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the chill of night remains,

As morning sunbeams dance, and warmth regains.

In Biratnagar, the air is thick with heat and scent,

A vibrant city, where the warm light is bent.

Aalborg's grey sky weeps with none, yet whispers low,

Of winter's chill, and winds that sharply blow.

The warmth of Biratnagar's sun-kissed streets I'd choose,

To bask in its radiance, and let my spirit loose.

The gentle warmth, the sweet scent of the air,

Invite me to linger, without a single care.

In Biratnagar, tomorrow's warmth will be my guide,

And in its light, my heart will reside.
