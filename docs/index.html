✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's warmth, a gentle breeze stirs,

As Biratnagar swelters, with winds that disperse.

Aalborg, chill and grey, with clouds that abide,

A stark contrast to the others, side by side.

The scent of wet earth rises in Kathmandu's air,

While Biratnagar's heat brings a parched, dry flair.

Aalborg's misty veil, a somber mood imparts,

But in Kathmandu's soft light, my heart finds its parts.
