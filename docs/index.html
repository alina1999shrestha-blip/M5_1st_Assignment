✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth awakes,

A pleasant day with clouds that softly part.

Biratnagar swelters, hot and dry, it makes

A stark contrast to the capital's gentle art.

Aalborg's chill begins to seep into the bone,

As winds howl strong, and skies are grey as stone.

The scent of wet earth rises in Kathmandu's air,

Inviting all to bask in its mild, serene care.

The soft light on its hills, a warm and cozy nest,

Makes it the city where I'd love to rest.
