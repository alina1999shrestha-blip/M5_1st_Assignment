✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth awakes,

As Biratnagar basks in heat that makes

The air thick with a heavy, languid scent,

While Aalborg's chill whispers a different intent.

The sky above Kathmandu, a brilliant blue,

Invites with a soft caress, both warm and new,

In contrast, Aalborg's grey sky weeps with dew,

And Biratnagar's haze hides the sun's warm view.

The gentle wind in Kathmandu beckons me,

To bask in its warm, golden light, wild and free.

Kathmandu's serene and peaceful atmosphere,

Makes it the nicest place to be, without a fear.
