✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As morning dawns, Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

With warmth that rises, as the sun begins to glow.

Biratnagar swelters, heat that clings to every place,

Aalborg's chill awakens, with a crisp and frosty face.

The skies above Kathmandu, a gentle haze do wear,

While Biratnagar's clouds gather, with a stormy air.

Aalborg's sky is open, with a light that dances free,

In Kathmandu's warm and gentle light, I'd love to be.
