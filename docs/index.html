✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the air is cool and grey,

A soothing breeze whispers through the Himalayan day.

Biratnagar's warmer skies, a gentle zephyr brings,

As sunbeams dance, and clouds congregate with gentle rings.

Aalborg's chill, a brisk awakening, crisp and light,

With scattered clouds, the Scandinavian sky takes flight.

The scent of rain in Kathmandu, a refreshing treat,

Invites me to wander, where the misty mountains meet.
