✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As Kathmandu's gentle breeze stirs the air,

Carrying scents of wet earth, beyond compare,

Biratnagar swelters, hot and dry and wide,

Its cloud cover thin, its sun beating with pride.

Aalborg's chill whispers secrets, crisp and light,

Its cloud-dappled sky a soothing, gentle sight.

The warmth of Biratnagar may appeal to some,

But tomorrow, Kathmandu's mildness will be my home.

The soft caress of its breeze, the gentle touch of its rain,

Make it the nicest place to be, free from summer's pain.
