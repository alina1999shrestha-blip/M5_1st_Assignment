✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the air is warm and still,

As Biratnagar swelters, shrouded in a similar chill.

Aalborg, by contrast, wears a gentler guise,

With sunlight dancing through its partly cloudy skies.

The breeze in Aalborg stirs, a gentle, cooling sigh,

As warmth and light entwine, a sensual, sweet surprise.

The scent of wet earth rises in Biratnagar's air,

While Kathmandu's chill whispers secrets, beyond compare.

Aalborg's mildness beckons, a haven from the heat,

A place to breathe, to live, where senses can retreat.
