✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As morning dawns, Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

With clouds that part to reveal a warm, sun-kissed glow.

In Biratnagar, the air is thick with heat and moist desire,

As rain drums down, a relentless, primal, summer fire.

Aalborg's sky, a deepened grey, with winds that cut like a knife,

Invites the chill, a crispness that awakens, full of life.

The warmth of Kathmandu, a haven from the stormy sight,

Beckons me to bask in its serene, and peaceful light.
