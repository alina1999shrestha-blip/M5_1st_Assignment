✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth awakes,

A soothing balance of chill and sunny breaks.

Biratnagar's sky, a deeper shade of grey,

Heavy with clouds, and scents of wet earth sway.

Aalborg's air, crisp and cold, with whispers low,

A chill that bites, and cloudy skies that glow.

Among these three, Kathmandu's warmth I'd choose,

To bask in mild sunlight, with a gentle breeze.
