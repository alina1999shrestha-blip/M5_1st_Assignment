✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu, the air is soft and cool,

with a gentle breeze that whispers through the trees,

a soothing calm that wraps the city in its gentle rule.

The clouds gather, a deep grey, with scent of rain that teases.

In Biratnagar, the sun beats down, a fiery blaze,

warming the skin, and lifting the heart to dazzling days.

Aalborg, in contrast, is crisp and cold,

with a chill that bites, and a sky that's pale and old.

But if I had to choose, I'd wander through Kathmandu's gentle haze,

where the warmth and coolness blend, in a sweet and soothing daze.
