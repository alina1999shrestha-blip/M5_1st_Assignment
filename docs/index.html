✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the air is warm and still,

With clouds that shroud the city, and a scent of wet earth's chill.

Biratnagar's breeze whispers low, a gentle, soothing sound,

As warmth awakens, and the sky is painted with a gentler cloud.

In Aalborg, the wind sweeps strong, with a crisp, cool light,

A chill that bites the skin, and a sky that's vast and bright.

The difference is stark, the sensations unique and wide,

But if I had to choose, I'd find my peace in Biratnagar's gentle tide.
