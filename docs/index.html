✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As morning breaks, Kathmandu's gentle breeze stirs,

with whispers of a warm day, tempered by cool airs.

In Biratnagar, the sun beats down, a fiery blaze,

with winds that sweep, and clouds that drift in haze.

Aalborg's chill dawn brings a misty, greyish light,

with gusts that cut, and skies that weep with gentle night.

The warmth of Biratnagar's day may be alluring still,

but Kathmandu's mild climate beckons, with a softer thrill.

The city's mix of sun and shade, a gentle, soothing sway,

invites the heart to linger, on this temperate day.
