✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the scent of wet earth rises,

As clouds veil the sky with a soft, grey disguise.

In Biratnagar, warmth abounds, with sunbeams dancing free,

A vibrant tapestry, woven with threads of light and glee.

Aalborg's chill whispers secrets, of a winter's lingering sigh,

As the wind stirs, with a mournful air, that beckons to the sky.

The contrast is stark, the choices few, yet one place stands apart,

Kathmandu's mild climate, a gentle warmth that touches the heart.
