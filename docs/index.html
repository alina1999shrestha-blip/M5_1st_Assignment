As morning dawns, Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

A soothing serenade, with temperatures gentle as a summer's glow.

In Biratnagar, warmth abounds, a sweltering heat that clings,

A world away from Aalborg's chill, where grey skies weep with rainy rings.

The contrast is stark, a tale of three cities, distinct and wide,

From Kathmandu's mild embrace to Biratnagar's sweltering inside,

Aalborg's biting wind and rain, a somber, wintry hue,

Kathmandu's gentle climate calls, a haven, pure and true.
