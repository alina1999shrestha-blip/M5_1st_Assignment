In Kathmandu, the sky is veiled in grey,

A soothing chill begins to seep and stay,

A gentle breeze stirs, with whispers of the rain to come, and the heart feels at ease.

The wind carries the sweet fragrance of blooming flowers, and the soul feels alive.

In Aalborg, the air is crisp, with a hint of winter's chill,

The light is pale, and the sky is a deep, foreboding still.

Kathmandu's mystique beckons, a serene and peaceful place to be, where the heart can unwind and be free.
