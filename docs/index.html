✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu, the sky is veiled, a deep grey hue,

Clouds cling to the mountains, heavy with rain anew.

Biratnagar swelters, warm winds whisper low,

A gentle breeze stirs, as the sun's heat starts to grow.

Aalborg's sky, a brilliant blue, with clouds that drift and play,

Invites the senses, with a cool, crisp air that's here to stay.

The chill of morning mist, the warmth of day's caress,

Make Aalborg's weather, a gentle, soothing bless.

The scent of wet earth rises, in Kathmandu's rainy night,

While Biratnagar's heat, and Aalborg's calm, make for a different delight.
