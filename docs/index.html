✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth awakes,

As Biratnagar's skies, with cloud cover, softly make

A contrasting scene, where sunshine filters through,

While Aalborg's chill, with grey skies, whispers anew.

The scent of wet earth rises in Aalborg's air,

As Kathmandu's mild winds, with whispers, gently share,

Biratnagar's heat, with a gentle, rustling sound,

Invites the senses, to a warmer ground.

The light, in Kathmandu, with a softer glow,

Makes it the haven, where the heart would want to go.
