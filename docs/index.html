In Kathmandu's misty veil, the air is warm and still,

With gentle breeze that whispers through the hill.

Biratnagar's skies, though cloudy, shine with heat,

As windswept streets resound with rustling feet.

Aalborg's chill, with gusts that cut like a knife,

Inviting all to bask in its gentle care.

Among these three, Kathmandu's warmth is just right,

A soothing balance of comfort and delight.
