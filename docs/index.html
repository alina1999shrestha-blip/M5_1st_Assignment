✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As morning breaks, Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

with warmth that rises, a soothing glow.

In Biratnagar, the sun beats strong and high,

amidst a restless wind that rustles by.

Aalborg's chill awakens, crisp and grey,

with a fierce gust that cuts, and drives the day.

The scent of wet earth rises in Biratnagar's air,

as Kathmandu's mild sky beckons, beyond compare.

The warmth and gentle breeze of Kathmandu's day,

make it the haven, where I'd choose to stay.
