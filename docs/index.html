As warmer breezes whisper through Kathmandu's streets,

A gentle caress that soothes the soul,

Biratnagar swelters, its heat a heavy weight,

While Aalborg shivers, lost in chill and grey.

The Himalayan city's mild and scented air,

Invites the heart to linger, without a care,

For though the cloud cover veils the sky,

Kathmandu's gentle warmth is where I'd reside.

The soft wind stirs, a melody so fine,

In Kathmandu, tomorrow's charm will be mine.
