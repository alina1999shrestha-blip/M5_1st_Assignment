✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the breeze whispers low,

A gentle chill that seeps beneath the skin, as clouds bestow

A soothing grayness, with precipitation's gentle touch.

In Biratnagar, warmth awakens, like a lover's tender clutch,

The scent of blooming flowers wafts, unbridled and free,

As sunbeams filter through the lighter sky, wild and carefree.

Aalborg's air is crisp and cold, with winds that keenly bite,

A somber mood that settles, like a shroud of winter's night.

But if I had to choose, I'd bask in Biratnagar's radiant light,

Where warmth and fragrance mingle, a sweet and heady delight.

The city's gentle breeze and scents would be my heart's retreat,

A place to bask in comfort, where love and joy would meet.
