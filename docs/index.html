✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As morning dawns, three cities stir,

Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

with whispers of a cloudy sky.

Biratnagar basks in warmer light,

where scents of blooms begin to rise.

Aalborg's chill winds howl, with grey skies weeping rain,

a stark contrast to the warmth that Biratnagar will gain.

The warmth of Biratnagar's sun-kissed streets,

invites the heart to wander, and to find retreat.

The city's gentle warmth, a haven from the chill,

awaits the traveler, with a soothing breeze to fill.
