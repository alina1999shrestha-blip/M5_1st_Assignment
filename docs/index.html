✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As morning dawns on Kathmandu's gentle breeze,

A mild warmth stirs, beneath a lightly veiled sky.

In Biratnagar, the heat intensifies with each degree,

A scorching max that beckons sun-kissed days to lie.

Aalborg, shrouded in a misty, chill disguise,

Faced with precipitation, and a wind that cuts like a sigh.

The scent of wet earth rises, as the sky weeps tears of rain,

While Kathmandu's soft light whispers sweet nothings, a gentle refrain.

The warmth of Biratnagar may lure, but for serene delight,

Kathmandu's balanced charm wins the heart, a gentle, peaceful night.
