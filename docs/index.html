As morning dawns, Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

A soothing calm, with clouds that softly glow.

In Biratnagar, warmth awakens, a vibrant, lively air,

With sunshine peeking through, and a gentle scent to share.

Aalborg, shrouded in mist, with winds that fiercely blow,

Feels chilled to the bone, with a grey sky that seems to grow.

The contrast is stark, the differences a sensory treat,

Kathmandu's mild climate beckons, a peaceful place to meet.
