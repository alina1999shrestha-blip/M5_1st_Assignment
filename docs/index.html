✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the air is warm and still,

A soothing breeze whispers secrets, as the clouds conceal the hill.

Biratnagar swelters, hot and dry, with winds that gently sway,

While Aalborg's sky is vast and wide, with a gentle chill of day.

The Danish city's breeze carries the scent of blooming flowers,

As sunbeams dance upon its face, in gentle, loving hours.

In contrast, Nepal's cities hide, beneath a cloudy, grey disguise,

But if I had to choose, I'd bask in Aalborg's gentle, sunny surprise.
