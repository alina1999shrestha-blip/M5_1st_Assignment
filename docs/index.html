✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the air is warm and still,

With scents of wet earth rising, a soothing gentle thrill.

Biratnagar's skies, a deeper blue, with winds that whisper low,

Invite the warmth of sunshine, as clouds drift by in tow.

Aalborg's breeze, a crisp caress, carries the chill of night,

Under skies of greyish hue, where stars shine with pale light.

The warmth of Biratnagar's sunbeams beckon me to stay,

Where the gentle rustle of leaves whispers a sweeter way.
