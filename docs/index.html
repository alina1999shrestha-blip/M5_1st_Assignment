In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the scent of petals sways,

As warmth awakens, and the chill of night fades away.

In Biratnagar, the sun beats down with fiery might,

While Aalborg's crisp air carries the whispers of a chilly night.

The skies above each city tell a different tale,

Kathmandu's clouds drift lazily, with a gentle, soothing gale.

Biratnagar's skies are vast and open, with a hint of desert dry,

Aalborg's blue expanse shines bright, with a freshness that catches the eye.

Of these three cities, Kathmandu's gentle touch is the most divine,

A place where warmth and coolness blend, in a delicate, sweet design.
