✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth awakes,

As Biratnagar basks in scorching, dry heat that shakes.

Aalborg's chill wraps tight, with winds that fiercely blow,

And rain-soaked skies that weep, in a dismal, grey show.

The scent of wet earth rises in Aalborg's air,

While Biratnagar's dry landscape stretches, devoid of care.

Kathmandu's mild temperature, a soothing balm to the skin,

Makes it the haven, where comfort and peace entwine within.
