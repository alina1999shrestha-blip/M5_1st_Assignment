✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu, the air is thick with heat and mist,

A soothing breeze stirs, with clouds that persist.

Biratnagar swelters, warm and dry to the bone,

As winds whisper secrets, beneath a partial throne.

Aalborg's chill caress, with windy skies so grey,

Invites the senses, to a different kind of day.

The scent of rain on earth, in Kathmandu fills the air,

A gentle warmth that beckons, beyond compare.
