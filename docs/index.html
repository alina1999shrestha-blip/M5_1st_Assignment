✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the warmth awakes,

A soothing balance of heat and cooling shades.

Biratnagar's sky, a deeper blue, invites with warmer rays,

As Aalborg's chill whispers winter's lingering sway.

The scent of blooming flowers in Kathmandu's air,

Contrasts with Aalborg's crisp, cold scent, beyond compare.

Biratnagar's heat, though intense, brings life to the ground,

But Kathmandu's mild climate makes it the nicest to be found.

The light dances upon its hills, a gentle, warm caress,

A haven from the cold, and the heat's intense excess.
