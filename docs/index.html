✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth awakes,

As cloud cover wraps the city in a soothing shade.

Biratnagar's higher skies, a deeper blue unfold,

With winds that whisper secrets, as the sun grows old.

Aalborg's chill, a contrast, with grey skies above,

A somber mood, with misty air, and a hint of love.

The scent of wet earth rises, in Kathmandu's air,

Inviting all to bask, in its mild, golden care.
