In Kathmandu's misty veil, the chill of night remains,

As Biratnagar basks in warmth, with breezy, sunny days.

Aalborg's sky is vast and wide, with clouds that drift away,

A gentle chill that whispers low, of winter's fading sway.

The scent of wet earth rises, in Kathmandu's morning air,

While Biratnagar's heat brings scents of blooming flowers to share.

Aalborg's crisp and clean, with a freshness that's hard to bear,

Kathmandu's mystique is calling, a gentle, soothing lair.
