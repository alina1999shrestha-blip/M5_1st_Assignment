✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As morning dawns, Kathmandu's misty veil ascends,

With gentle breeze that stirs the scent of wet earth that never ends.

In Biratnagar, the sun beats strong and free,

Warming the skin, with a light that's wild and carefree.

Aalborg's chill remains, with a sky that's grey and wide,

A contrast to the warmth that Biratnagar cannot hide.

The warmth of Biratnagar calls, with its light and airy space,

A haven from the chill, a perfect place.
