✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's veil of clouds, the breeze whispers low,

a gentle warmth that soothes the soul, with scents of wet earth rising.

Biratnagar's sky, a deeper shade, with winds that rustle, free and light,

contrasts with Aalborg's chill, where northern breezes bite.

The Danish city's clouded sky, a somber hue, with air crisp and cold,

invites a different kind of peace, one that's calm and old.

But as the heart yearns for a gentle clime, with warmth and scent of bloom,

Kathmandu's soft and misty veil, beckons, a serene perfume.
