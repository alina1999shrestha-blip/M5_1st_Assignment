✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the breeze whispers low,

A gentle caress that soothes the skin, as clouds bestow

A soft, grey light upon the city's face.

In Biratnagar, warmth awakens, a vibrant, pulsing pace,

The air thick with scent, as petals unfold, and precipitation falls.

Aalborg, in contrast, wears a chill, its wind a biting call,

The sky a pale, wintry hue, with clouds that drift and sway.

Of these three cities, Biratnagar's warmth would be the nicest to stay.
