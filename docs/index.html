As Kathmandu awakens to a gentle breeze,

With temperatures that dance between fourteen and twenty-four degrees,

The cloud cover whispers secrets, a gentle hue of grey.

Biratnagar beckons with warmth, its max thirty-one degrees in May,

Aalborg, in contrast, wears a cloak of chill, with winds that sway,

A city shrouded in mist, where the air is crisp and grey.

The scent of Kathmandu's valleys, a mix of earth and green,

Invites me to wander, where the light and air are serene.
