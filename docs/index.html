In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth awakes,

As sunbeams filter through the morning's haze.

In Biratnagar, the heat intensifies,

With cloudless skies and a burning surprise.

Aalborg's chill whispers a different tale,

With grey skies weeping mist, and a gentle gale.

The scent of damp earth rises from the ground,

In Aalborg's misty veil, nature's peace is found.

But if I had to choose, I'd bask in Kathmandu's light,

Where warmth and gentle winds dance through the night.
