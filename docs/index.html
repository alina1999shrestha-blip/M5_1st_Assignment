✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the air is warm and sweet,

With scents of blooming flowers beneath the cloudy street.

Biratnagar's skies, a deeper blue, with sunshine warm and bright,

Invite the senses to a lively, vibrant delight.

Aalborg's chill, a crisp breeze stirs, with clouds that drift and play,

A cooler haven from the heat of the other two's warm day.

The gentle wind in Kathmandu whispers low, a soothing serenade,

Making it the nicest place to be, in this warm and cloudy shade.
