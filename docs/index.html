✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

As Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

A soft caress on skin, with warmth to show,

Biratnagar's heat intensifies the air,

A vibrant pulse, with life beyond compare.

Aalborg's chill, a crisp and icy bite,

Contrasts with the others, in morning light,

The sky above, a deep and endless blue,

Invites to stroll, with scent of blooms anew.

In Kathmandu, the balance is just right,

A pleasant warmth, that feels like delight.
