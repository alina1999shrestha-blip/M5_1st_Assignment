✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, a gentle breeze whispers low,

as clouds conceal the sun, and petals unfold to misty dew.

Biratnagar's warm skies beckon, with a gentle, scented air,

where sunbeams dance and leaves rustle, without a care.

Aalborg's grey horizon stretches, with a chill that bites the skin,

and clouds that weep with sorrow, in a melancholy din.

Tomorrow, I would wander in Biratnagar's golden light,

where warmth and scent entwine, and the heart takes gentle flight.

The city's vibrant pulse, a rhythm that resonates with ease,

invites me to linger, and let the warmth of the day release.
