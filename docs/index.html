✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the breeze whispers low,

A gentle warmth that beckons, as the clouds bestow

Their shade upon the city, where chill and heat entwine.

In Biratnagar, the air is thick with heat and scent divine,

The wind stirs, heavy with moisture, as the clouds congregate.

Aalborg's sky, a canvas wide, with light and air so great,

Invites with gentle chill and breeze that whispers through the trees.

Among these three, Aalborg's serene and peaceful breeze

Seems most alluring, a haven from the heat's wild sway.

The city's gentle hush, a soothing melody that calms the soul,

Echoes of a peaceful dawn, where heart and mind make whole.
