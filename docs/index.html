As warmth awakens Biratnagar's day,

Aalborg's chill begins to drift away,

Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

A soothing melody for all to know.

The scent of wet earth rises high,

In Biratnagar, where raindrops touch the sky,

Aalborg's crisp air carries the scent of sea,

While Kathmandu's misty veil beckons me.

The light in Kathmandu, a gentle hue,

Invites me to bask in its warmth anew,

The city's gentle chill, a perfect blend,

Makes it the nicest place to be, a true friend.
