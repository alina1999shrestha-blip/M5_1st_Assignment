✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the sun's warmth starts to seep,

A soothing balance of chill and heat, the day to keep.

Biratnagar's sky, a deeper blue, with clouds that lazily roam,

Invites with a warmer, more languid tone.

Aalborg, in contrast, faces a chill, grey, windy day,

With rain that whispers secrets, as the clouds drift by in disarray.

The scent of wet earth rises, a primal, wild perfume,

But for comfort, Kathmandu's gentle air is where I'd assume.
