In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the air is crisp and light,

A soothing chill that whispers through the night.

Where scents of blooming flowers dance and sway.

But Biratnagar's warmth beckons, and I'd rather be there to try.

The city's gentle heat, a haven from the chill,

Invites me to bask in its radiant, golden light still.
