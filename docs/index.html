✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's misty veil, the breeze whispers low,

With cloud cover high, the chill begins to seep and flow.

Biratnagar's warmer touch, a gentle caress on skin,

Invites with scents of blooming flowers, and a sky within.

Aalborg's crisp air awakens, with a light that dances free,

Under open skies, the warmth of sunshine beckons me.

The contrast is stark, yet each city's charm is unique and true,

But tomorrow, I'd bask in Biratnagar's warmth, with a heart anew.
