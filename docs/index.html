✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the chill of night still clings,

As morning sunbeams warm the streets with gentle rings.

Biratnagar swelters, hot and dry, with winds that whip and sway,

While Aalborg's cloudless sky shines bright, in a warmer, drier way.

The scent of wet earth rises in Biratnagar's heavy air,

As precipitation falls, and thunderous clouds gather there.

In contrast, Aalborg's light and warmth invite, with a gentle, caressing breeze,

Making it the nicest place to be, with a serene and peaceful ease.
