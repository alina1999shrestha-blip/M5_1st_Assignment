✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's valleys, a gentle breeze stirs,

with warm sunlight filtering through the trees.

Biratnagar swelters, its heat unabated,

a stark contrast to Aalborg's chill, grey seas.

The Danish city's winds whip fiercely, cold and strong,

while Kathmandu's scent of wet earth rises, sweet and long.

The sky above Biratnagar shines with partial light,

a compromise between sun and cloud's soft, white.
