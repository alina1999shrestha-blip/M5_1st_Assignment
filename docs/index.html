In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, the sun's warmth whispers low,

A soothing calm that beckons, as the clouds drift by and go.

In Biratnagar, heat waves shimmer, a scorching air that clings,

While Aalborg's windy chill, a dampness that the senses sting.

The Danish city's sky is veiled, a grey mist that won't depart,

In contrast, Biratnagar's blue expanse, a radiant, sunny heart.

Kathmandu's mild temperature, a haven from the extremes,

Makes it the perfect haven, where comfort and peace it seems.
