✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth whispers low,

A soothing melody that only summer knows.

Biratnagar's heat waves dance, a fiery, sweltering beat,

As Aalborg's chill winds howl, with rain's relentless repeat.

The skies, a deepening grey, in all three cities meet,

Yet, in Kathmandu, the air is sweet, with scents so discreet.

The warmth on skin, a comfort that's hard to beat,

Makes Kathmandu the haven, where heart and soul can meet.
