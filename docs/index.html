✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu, the air is thick with rain,

A deluge that drums against the rooftops' refrain.

Biratnagar swelters, a humid, heavy space,

Where warmth clings close, a sticky, sweet embrace.

Aalborg's sky, a canvas of patchy grey and blue,

Invites with gentle breezes, a soothing calm anew.

The chill of morning lingers, a refreshing, crisp scent,

Making Aalborg the haven where I'd love to bent.

The warmth of Biratnagar and the rain of Kathmandu aside,

Aalborg's gentle touch is where I'd choose to reside.
