In Kathmandu's gentle breeze, warmth whispers low,

A soothing balance of heat and gentle glow.

Biratnagar blazes, a scorching summer's day,

With winds that whip and sunbeams that sway.

And winds that howl, a somber, wintry way.

The scent of rain in Kathmandu's air prevails,

Inviting all to bask in its gentle, warm regales.

Kathmandu's soft light beckons, a haven from the heat,

A place to find solace, where warmth and calm meet.
