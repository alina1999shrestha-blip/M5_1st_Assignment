As morning dawns, Kathmandu's gentle breeze whispers low,

with clouds that veil the sky, and mist that softly flows.

In Biratnagar, warmth abounds, with sunbeams that ignite,

a sweltering heat that wraps the city in its burning light.

and skies that stretch out grey, with not a cloud in sight.

The scent of wet earth rises in Kathmandu's air,

inviting all to bask in its mild and soothing care.

Among the three, Kathmandu's gentle charm is hard to resist,

a haven from the heat and cold, where comfort and peace persist.
