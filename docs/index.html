✍️ Poem — English & Nepali

In Kathmandu, a gentle breeze stirs the air,

As warmth and chill entwine, beyond compare.

The sky is vast, with clouds that drift and play,

A soothing calm, to start the day.

In Biratnagar, the sun beats down with might,

A scorching heat, that banishes the night.

In Aalborg, grey skies weep with cloudy tears,

A somber mood, that calms all fears.

But if I had to choose, where to roam,

I'd find my peace, in Kathmandu's gentle home.
